Q8: Calculate the heat generated in a electric heater of 1000 watt, 240 volt, worked for 5 minutes.

  • 70.5 Kilo calories
  • 71.0 Kilo calories
  • 71.6 Kilo calories
  • 72.1 Kilo calories
Show Calculation
\(P = 1000 \, \text{W}\) \(t = 5 \, \text{minutes} = 5 \times 60 = 300 \, \text{seconds}\) \(1 \, \text{kcal} = 4184 \, \text{J}\) \[ Q = P \times t = 1000 \times 300 = 300{,}000 \, \text{J} \] \[ Q = \frac{300{,}000}{4184} \approx 71.7 \, \text{kcal} \] \[ \boxed{71.7 \, \text{kcal}} \]
